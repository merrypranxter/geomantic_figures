# geomantic_figures

> 16 figures. 4 lines of 1 or 2 dots. via. populus. conjunctio. puella. each figure has an elemental composition, a planetary ruler, a meaning. the shield chart: 4 mothers -> 4 daughters -> 4 nephews -> 4 court figures. earth divination by dot pattern.

**Archetype:** GPGPU GRID SIM. **Distinct from** `cellular_automata` (not state evolution) and `iching_hexagrams` (6-line vs 4-line).

## Adjacency
- `iching_hexagrams` — binary divination cousin, different bit depth
- `abelian_sandpile` — grid-based pattern relaxation
- `dla_clustering` — random dot aggregation

## Visual Targets
- 16 figures as 4-line dot patterns (1 or 2 dots per line)
- Shield chart: 16-figure layout with generational flow
- House chart: 12 astrological houses with figure allocation
- Elemental coloring: fire=red, air=yellow, water=blue, earth=black
- Figure generation: mothers -> daughters (line inversion) -> nephews (addition) -> court

## Technical Notes
- 4-bit encoding: 0=1 dot, 1=2 dots per line
- Standard figure names and correspondences
- Shield chart generation algorithm from 4 random mothers
- House allocation: figures distributed to 12 houses by specific rules
